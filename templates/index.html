How It Works

Four specialized AI agents work together to analyze every citation in your paper.

1

Citation Extractor

Extracts all citations using regex pattern matching and LLM analysis

2

Context Analyzer

Understands what claim each citation is supposed to support

3

Source Verifier

Checks if cited sources plausibly exist and support the claims

4

Hallucination Detector

Cross-references all evidence to flag fabricated citations
